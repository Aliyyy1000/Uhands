Mission, Vision & Objectives

Our Mission

To drive transformative change in Nigerian communities by empowering vulnerable women and children, improving access to health, education, governance and livelihoods.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where vulnerable communities thrive with resilience, dignity, and equal opportunities for sustainable development.

Our Objectives

Promoting climate action, healthcare access, quality education, transparent governance, sustainable agriculture, and the rights and well-being of women and children.

What We Do

UHANDS runs community-led programs focused on measurable impact in climate, health, education, governance and livelihoods.

Climate Resilience

Small-scale renewable projects, flood mitigation and community training to adapt to climate change.

Healthcare Access

Mobile clinics, maternal health education, immunization drives and health-worker training.

Education & Skills

After-school programs, vocational training for women, and scholarships for vulnerable children.
