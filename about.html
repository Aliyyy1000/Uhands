About UHANDS

UHANDS exists to uplift vulnerable communities across Nigeria by focusing on climate resilience, health, education, governance and livelihoods. We emphasize community leadership, local partnerships and measurable impact.

Objective & Mission

To empower vulnerable communities, particularly women and children, in Nigeria by addressing critical issues such as climate change, health, education, governance, livelihood, and agriculture — improving quality of life and promoting sustainable development.

Principles & Approach

  • Community-led planning and ownership
  • Transparency and accountability
  • Sustainability and local capacity building
